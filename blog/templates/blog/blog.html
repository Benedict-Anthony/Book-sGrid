Welcome to your favourite book/blog API

api/posts/

this gives you all the books

api/posts/"slug"/

this gives you all the single book

api/posts/"params"/

this allows you to search for books

api/posts/slug/"related"/

this gives you the all the books that are related to the single book you quaried

api/"authors"/

Here you are able to get all the authors in our blog

api/authors/"url"/

This gives you a single author and its profile

api/authors/"posts"/

Here you are able to get all the post made by a single in our blog

Note: All this end point only accepts a get request!
